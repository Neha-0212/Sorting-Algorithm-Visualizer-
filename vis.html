What is VISUALIZER?

A sort visualizer is a feature that enables us to observe sorting algorithms in action. It typically includes an intuitive user interface with controls for selecting sorting algorithms, and adjusting visualization settings. We can choose from various sorting algorithms and watch as the chosen algorithm rearranges the data in real-time or through step-by-step animations. The design focuses on providing a clear and interactive experience, with responsive layout and controls for customization.

The sort visualizer enhances understanding of sorting algorithms by allowing users to visually explore their functionality.
